Attractions

Enjoy year-round activities in dance, theater, visual arts and environmental education at the National Park for the Arts. Take a ride on an antique carousel, have a picnic, learn a new dance style in the Spanish Ballroom, or attend an art show or special event.

Recreation

The Capital Crescent Trail runs right through Downtown Bethesda and extends from Georgetown in DC to Silver Spring, Maryland. The paved trail is a popular recreation spot to enjoy walking, jogging, biking, or rollerblading.

Special Events

Special events are held in Bethesda throughout the year. Here are some of the most popular annual events:

  • Bethesda Literary Festival
  • Bethesda Fine Arts Festival
  • Imagination Bethesda
  • Bethesda Outdoor Movies
